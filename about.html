About Malach

Malach was born on June 5th, 1999, 19 years before this site was actualized.
He was born to Karni Dorell and Doug Campbell, two Red Hook based artists.
He follows in their footsteps in some respects, as he is an accomplished visual artist in many mediums, be it drawing, collage, photography, or even Photoshop doodles.
In the 1st grade, Malach met probably the single greatest influence in his life (although that is a story for another page).
Malach is:

  • Funny
  • Quick-witted
  • Charismatic
  • Handsome
  • A Great Storyteller and Listener
  • Good at Making Mixes
  • A Good Drinking/Cooking Buddy
  • In All Actuality My Closest Friend
  • Kind of Shitty
All in all, he has come into his own in a lot of ways, and while he's made a lot of progress in his life from when I've first met him, he has a lot more to go through - college bullshit, romantic bullshit, miscellaneous uncategorized bullshit; either way, he's grown into someone I like to call my friend and plan to call so for as long as I can foresee.
